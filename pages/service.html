After-sale Service

Thoughtful Warranty & Full-Cycle Customer Support

1. Warranty Policy

  • The F87 keyboard comes with a 24-month nationwide official warranty; free repair and spare part replacement for non-artificial failures within warranty coverage.
  • Free replacement for factory defects within 7 days after receipt; free product exchange for quality issues within 15 days.
  • Damage caused by liquid ingress, dropping or unauthorized disassembly is excluded from free warranty service; repair is available at component cost price.

2. Consultation Channels

  • Online Service: Official store customer service on all sales platforms, available 9:00 - 22:00.
  • Hotline: Official 400 after-sales hotline, working hours 8:30 - 18:00 on workdays.
  • Website Ticket: Submit service tickets on official site; our support team will reply within 24 hours.

E-mail: service@tarantula-key.com | Service Hotline: 400-XXXX-888

3. Value-added Services

  • Lifetime free download and upgrade of official driver software for all F87 users.
  • Original replacement accessories including keycaps, switches and data cables are available for purchase on our official store.

4. Repair & Logistics Rules

  • For valid warranty repairs due to product defects: all round-trip shipping fees are covered by manufacturer.
  • For non-warranty man-made damage repair: customers only cover outgoing shipping cost; return delivery is free from our factory.
  • 12 official offline maintenance centers nationwide; in-person inspection available in major first & second-tier cities.
  • All failure data collected from after-sales cases will be shared with R&D team for continuous product optimization and iteration.
