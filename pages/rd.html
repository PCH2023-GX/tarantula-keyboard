R & D Center

Innovation & Technology for Better Keyboards

1. Five R&D Groups

  • Structural Design: F87 shell, inner tank, positioning plate mold opening, ergonomic optimization.
  • Circuit Team: PCB mainboard & wireless module design, reduce keyboard delay and power consumption.
  • Switch Tuning: Custom exclusive switches for F87: gaming, silent and tactile switches.
  • Driver Development: Official driver for macro programming and RGB lighting customization.
  • Testing Team: Full reliability tests: high-low temp, plug, drop and power aging test.

2. F87 Self-developed Core Technology

  • Total R&D investment: 8 million RMB, 7 authorized utility model patents.
  • Full-key 5-pin hot-swap base, compatible with most 3/5-pin custom switches.
  • In-house 2.4G wireless solution with latency＜8ms, ahead of same-price industry level.
  • 3-layer cavity filler: sandwich foam + switch pad + bottom foam to eliminate hollow sound.

3. Product R&D Process

Full Flow:Project → Draft → 3D Sample → Trial Run → Circuit Debug → Internal Test → Revision → Small Batch → QC → Mass Production

  • F87 finished 12 structural revisions and 9 PCB version updates before finalization.
  • Firmware & driver updated every two months based on global user feedback.

4. Sustainable Innovation

We keep continuous annual R&D investment to launch new products & advanced technologies, satisfying global gamers and office users’ demands.
