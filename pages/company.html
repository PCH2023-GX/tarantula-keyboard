About Tarantula Electronics

Tarantula Electronics has long been engaged in R&D, production and sales of mechanical keyboards, focusing on cost-effective customized mechanical keyboard development.

1. Basic Company Information

Founded in 2012, Tarantula is located in Dongguan High-tech Industrial Park, covering an area of 32,000 square meters.

It has five departments: R&D, Production, QC, Domestic Sales and Overseas Sales, with over 1200 staff including 186 professional R&D engineers.

Our goods sell nationwide and export to more than 30 countries across Europe, America and Southeast Asia, cooperating with over 6000 offline digital retail stores.

2. Company Development Timeline

  • 2012: Brand registered, first blue-switch mechanical keyboard launched.
  • 2015: Self-built factory, independent keyboard production instead of OEM.
  • 2018: R&D lab established for hot-swappable & wireless keyboard technology.
  • 2022: F87 project launched after 18 months of structural optimization.
  • 2024: Mass production of F87 series becomes our flagship product line.

3. Business Philosophy & Honors

Core principle: Affordable customized keyboards with strict material control to cut unnecessary premium cost.

We sponsor e-sports competitions and university research cooperation programs.

Certified as High-tech Enterprise with multiple authorized patents for peripheral hardware invention.
